Janet Sri Lanka

Janet Sri Lanka represents a heritage of natural beauty, and my goal was to translate that botanical essence into a dynamic digital experience. Through fluid motion graphics and a refined visual palette, we transformed traditional brand values into a contemporary narrative that breathes life into their product line.

overview

I developed a comprehensive motion design system focused on "organic flow," mimicking the natural ingredients found in Janet products. By utilizing advanced 2D and 3D animation techniques, we reduced visual clutter and enhanced product storytelling, ensuring that every transition felt as smooth as the skincare it represents.

The reimagined visual identity for Janet Sri Lanka serves as a powerful engagement tool. By integrating motion-first storytelling, we increased time-on-page and successfully bridged the gap between heritage skincare and a younger, digitally-native audience.
